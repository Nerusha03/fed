Introduction

Originally part of Weekly Shonen Jump, BLEACH captured hearts worldwide, selling over 130 million manga volumes.

The anime's 366 episodes began in 2004, leading to four feature films. Now, the final arc, Thousand-Year Blood War, is unfolding.

Now, “Thousand-Year Blood War”, the concluding arc of the iconic anime, is finally about to unfold.

For the occasion, eminent anime maestro Tomohisa Taguchi brings his unique visual sense as Series Director and Series Composition. Joining the team are BLEACH anime veterans Shiro SAGISU (musician) and Masashi Kudo (character designer). And to bring it all to life once again is studio Pierrot for animation production. The team of talented veterans is ready for the final showdown.

What awaits Ichigo Kurosaki?
