Introduction

Originally part of Weekly Shonen Jump, BLEACH captured hearts worldwide, selling over 130 million manga volumes.

The anime's 366 episodes began in 2004, leading to four feature films. Now, the final arc, Thousand-Year Blood War, is unfolding.

Directed by Tomohisa Taguchi, with music by Shiro SAGISU, and animation by studio Pierrot.
